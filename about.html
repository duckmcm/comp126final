So this website is essentially practice for my internship with the Grounds Department next semester. One of my main tasks is to create a website for the Battle Grove Stream Restoration, the focus of which is to be on helping visitors learn about native pollinators. Eventually, under the guidance of my boss, I'll fill out the insect database the "Field Guide Search" page will link to. But, that's atill a ways off yet.

By the way, both the honeycomb texture that's used as the background and the bee photograph above/to the left are my own work! That's why you don't see any attributions for them. (I took the honeycomb pictures while the beekeeping club was figuring out how to process beeswax - we lost both our hives, but we made a LOT of candles!)
